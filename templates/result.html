Know More, Count Better!

Viscount is a video upload-based crowd monitoring platform designed to improve public safety and operational efficiency in public spaces, events, and commercial facilities. With a combination of automated video analytics and an intuitive reporting interface, Viscount turns recordings into actionable insights.

Tutorials

1. Upload Video

Select the “Upload Video” menu, click “Select File,” then press Upload.

2. Analysis Process

The system will analyze crowd density and flow. This process usually takes a few minutes depending on the video duration.

3. View Results

Open the “Analysis Results” menu to view density visuals, crowd statistics, and summary reports.

4. Use the Information

Use the analysis results to evaluate density, manage areas, and improve visitor safety.
